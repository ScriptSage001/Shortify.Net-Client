About Us - Shortify.NET
Who We Are
Shortify.NET is a fully open-source URL-shortening application independently initiated and maintained by a single developer. Created out of a passion for simplifying online link sharing, this project is designed to provide a user-friendly and feature-rich platform accessible to everyone. With all the code made public, Shortify.NET is a transparent and collaborative endeavor aimed at empowering individuals and businesses alike.
Our Mission
At Shortify.NET, our mission is to:
  • Simplify Sharing: Empower users to create and share links effortlessly
  • Foster Collaboration: Promote community-driven development through open-source contributions.
  • Empower Users: Provide advanced tools for analytics and link management.
Our Vision
We envision a future where URL shortening is not just a utility but a tool for smarter online interactions. With an open-source foundation, Shortify.NET aims to build a community that innovates together.
Meet the Creator
Shortify.NET is developed and maintained by Kaustab Samanta, a passionate full-stack software developer from India, specializing in .NET Core and Angular. This project is a testament to the possibilities of individual creativity and dedication.
