Getting Started
How to Create a Short URL
Shortify.NET makes it easy to create short URLs in just a few steps:
  1. Sign in to your account.
  2. Click on the Create New / Create Link button.
  3. Paste your long URL into the Destination field.
  4. Optionally, add tags or a title for better organization.
  5. Click Create. Your short URL is ready to share!

Sharing Your Short URL
Sharing your shortened URL is simple:
  • Copy the generated short URL from the Dashboard, Details screen or Share popup.
  • Share it via email, social media, or messaging platforms.
  • Use the Share button on the Dashboard or Details screen for direct sharing options.
Editing URL Details
Keep your URLs updated with accurate details:
  1. Navigate to the Links section.
  2. Locate the URL you want to edit and click the Edit button (Pencil icon).
  3. Update the Title, Tags, or Destination URL as needed.
  4. Save your changes to keep everything organized.
  5. Additionaly you can edit the URL details from the Detail screen as well.
Understanding URL Analytics (Coming Soon)
Track your URLs' performance with the analytics feature:
  • Click Metrics: See how many people clicked on your short URLs.
  • Geographic Data: Know where your audience is located.
  • Referrers Insights: Identify the most popular websites or platforms your URLs are accessed from.
Role-Based Access Control
Shortify.NET has two roles:
  • Customer: Default role for all users; access basic features like URL creation and analytics.
  • Admin: Advanced permissions for managing users and monitoring system-wide performance.
